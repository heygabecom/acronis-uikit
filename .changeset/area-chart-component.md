Add `AreaChart` — a typed recharts composition over the shared `Chart` primitives, with `layout` (single / stacked) and `fill` (solid / gradient) variants, a `curve` control, per-point dots, null-gap bridging, and a themed tooltip/legend/axes/grid with caller-supplied series colors. Single vs multi area follows from the number of `dataKeys`. Initial version ported from the apps/demo `AreaChartPlayground`; design + data-viz palette reconciliation pending.
